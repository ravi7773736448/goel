Who We Are

Built on Precision & Trust

Established in 1973, Goel Enterprises has been developing and manufacturing a full range of Fasteners, Scaffolding, Nuts, Bolts, Rivets, Washers, Thread Bar type Hinges and all sorts of Machine Screws.

Our products are well established and preferred especially in the Middle East, Saudi Arabia, Gulf countries and many African countries. We maintain the highest quality at every stage with our own R&D department.
